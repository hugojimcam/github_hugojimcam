Final Project Updates

For my project, I aim to create a music collection using p5.js. The user will be able to press a key, and a song will start playing, accompanied by the album cover. I have just started figuring out which songs I would like to feature and downloading them. I used this website to find the images to download for free (https://bendodson.com/projects/itunes-artwork-finder/). Currently, the collection will feature a variety of songs I enjoy, but I may also include a theme, such as the evolution of rock music or rap.
